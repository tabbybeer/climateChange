How climate change can be avoided

The devastating effects of climate change include increases in pests and diseases and more frequent and intense droughts and floods which reduces the availability of food. Similarly, heat-stress causes poor yields, or worse, crop failures. Reducing short-lived climate pollutants gives us our best chance to rapidly limit global temperature rise and reduce these risks to food security.

Ways to avoid

  1. Reduce meat consumption
  2. Reduce electricity and water usage
  3. Recycle
  4. Use renewable energy sources
